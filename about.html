about

giddyup digital is a small website design and development company based out of Bend Oregon, specializing in helping small businesses and entrepenuers succeed in a digitized world.

Hello, my name is Morgan.

While transitioning my career from Mechanical to Software engineering, I began designing and developing websites as a way to earn money while also expanding my skill set. What started as a way to make ends meet quickly morphed into something more, as I found a deep sense of meaning from being able to help small business owners reach new heights through the internet. I decided to chase this passion and pursue web design and development full time, and giddyup digital llc was born.
