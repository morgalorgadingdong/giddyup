case study

objective

Madaesthetics sought a new website to help strengthen their brand’s digital presence, allow them to sell product online, and create a digital version of their popular acne bootcamp.

Online Store

Madaesthetics needed an online store that integrated with their existing Square account, which they used for payment processing and inventory management. We sync’d up with Square’s backend to create an online store which is 100% maintainable through their existing square account, allowing for a seamless evolution from in person to online sales management.

Online Bootcamp

Madaesthetics Acne bootcamps are a 12 week acne program designed to give clients the tools and knowledge to have healthy skin for life. Madaesthetics wanted to create a digital version of their acne bootcamp, where they could still provide clients with customized skin care recommendations and products without the in person treatments. We built for them an online bootcamp application that allows clients to subscribe, upload photos and questionnaire response, and recieve a personalized skin care regimen.
